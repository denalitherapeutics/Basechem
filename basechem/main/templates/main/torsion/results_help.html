Torsion Tips
  • Click "Show" to display a compound's torsion scan graph in the chart.
  • The vertical line in each chart shows the dihedral of the submitted conformation.
  • Click points on the graph to add and remove poses from the viewer.
  • To remove poses from the viewer, click on the "Hide" button or deselect a point from the graph.
  • "Lowest Energy" selects the lowest energy pose of each shown compound.
  • "Match Dihedral" is only available when there is a single pose/dihedral in the viewer. Clicking the button will display the pose matching the chosen dihedral for each compound in the chart.
  • The "Lowest Energy" and "Match Dihedral" buttons will only affect compounds that appear in the chart.
If you have more questions about how this works, check out the FAQ and/or contact MnI!
